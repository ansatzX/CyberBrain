# 阅读论文

基于三份写作指南，独立呈现。

## 来源 A：Mack，《How to Write a Good Scientific Paper》（SPIE Press, 2018）

已读章节：Ch1（入门）、Ch2（结构与组织）、Ch3（语言与风格）、Ch6（摘要与标题）、Ch7（编辑视角）。

### A.1 IMRAD 结构（Ch. 2）

Mack 的期刊样本中 78% 的论文使用了 IMRAD 的某种变体。Mack 也给出了一个替代框架：CEC（主张、证据、评述，claim, evidence, commentary）。

**引言。** Mack 引用了 Swales 的三步模型：（1）确立领域——该领域是什么，为何重要，已有何成果；（2）确立空白——指出 gap、提出问题或挑战已有工作；（3）占据该空白——概述目的，宣布本研究，可选地总结结果。常见陷阱：不必要的背景（告诉读者他们已经知道的）、夸大重要性、未能明确论文试图回答哪些研究问题。

**方法。** 两个目标而非一个：实现可复现性，以及使读者能够**判断结果的有效性**。内部有效性 = 结论由所呈现的结果支持。外部有效性 = 可推广性。Mack 明确批评了对可复现性的放弃："Rarely does a method section explain why one approach was chosen over another. Nobody reproduces other people's work anymore, or so the thought goes. I find this attitude mistaken and often self-serving." 常见陷阱：在方法中包含结果、无关细节、按时间顺序而非逻辑顺序叙述。"If the raw results are not going to be presented, then a description of the data-reduction procedures is required."

**结果与讨论。** 结果部分"should be very short. It is simply a presentation of the results obtained." 必须"clearly designate those results that are new (never before published)." Mack 的关键警告：**"Evidence does not explain itself."** 讨论部分反转引言结构（从特殊到一般）。阶段：总结结果、讨论结果是预期还是意外、与先前工作比较、解释/说明、对普适性提出假设。"Any problems or shortcomings encountered during the course of the work should also be discussed." 常见陷阱：忽略矛盾结果、"drawing conclusions from results without sound logical arguments to back them up."

**结论。** "Should be more than a summary." 提供"the most general claims that can be supported by the evidence." 推测仅在"clearly labeled as speculative"时方可接受。常见陷阱：重复摘要、引入新证据。

### A.2 科学风格（Ch. 3）

Mack 通过五个立场定义科学风格（源自 Thomas & Turner）：

| 立场 | 定义 |
|---|---|
| 真理（Truth） | 客观实在独立于作者/读者而存在。可发现、可验证。作者不声称特权通道。 |
| 呈现（Presentation） | 价值排序：准确性 > 精确性 > 清晰性 > 简洁性 > 优雅性。"Accuracy means that all new knowledge claims are justified and verifiable." 夸大主张的两种方式：与事实相反的主张、无事实支持的主张。"Any claim that cannot be verified given the information provided in the paper is as good as unsupported." |
| 场景（Scene） | 一场学术报告——听众对该主题感兴趣，会把问题留到最后。正式、专业语气。 |
| 受众（Cast） | 同行：聪明、愿意投入精力，但仅当作者值得他们这样做。"Sometimes experts in the specific niche...but usually not." |
| 思想与语言（Thought and Language） | 语言（包括数学）完全胜任。"Sloppy use of words is considered a sign of sloppy thinking." |

关于被动语态："the scientific stance on truth makes the scientist replaceable; anybody could have done the same experiments." 第一人称代词在作者提出建议或倡议时是合适的——这些行为中作者不可替代。

### A.3 摘要与标题（Ch. 6）

Mack 将摘要和标题框架为**匹配**——将正确的论文送到正确的读者手中。他倡导结构化摘要，包含五个部分：背景（Background）、目的（Aim）、方法（Approach）、结果（Results）、结论（Conclusions）。先写成结构化摘要，然后删除小标题合并为一段。

Mack 引用了一项对六种主要医学期刊的 1999 年研究：约 **40% 的摘要包含与正文不一致的信息**。他的三部分摘要检验：（1）摘要中的所有信息是否与正文一致？（2）摘要中的所有信息是否都能在正文中找到？（3）论文的重要信息是否在摘要中？

摘要的目的不是"推销"论文——而是让**正确**的读者读到它。大多数论文（99.9%）不应被大多数读者阅读。匹配关乎精度，而非数量。

### A.4 编辑标准（Ch. 7）

编辑使用的四个标准：

1. **范围（Scope）**：内容是否符合期刊范围？
2. **质量（Quality）**：两个方面——工作的质量，以及写作的质量。"if the initial quality of the writing is not sufficiently high, it may be nearly impossible to judge the quality of the work itself."
3. **新颖性（Novelty）**：至少 50% 的结果应该是新的（Mack 的经验法则）。"An effective literature search and comprehensive citations are a requirement to establish what about the submitted work is novel."
4. **重要性（Significance）**：问题重要性 × 进展幅度。"Even a small advance in a topic that hundreds or thousands of readers care about can be considered significant."

Mack 也讨论了**零结果偏见**："In science, the only failed experiment is one that does not lead to a conclusion." 期刊、审稿人和作者都偏爱阳性结果。作者投稿偏见是发表偏见的绝大部分原因——阴性结果从不被投稿。

### A.5 Mack 覆盖的论文类型

Mack 专注于标准研究论文。他没有关于综述、方法论文、基准论文、基金申请书或学位论文的单独章节。Ch. 2 中他提到"For some research articles, it is the method that is novel. For this case, a much more detailed description is required"是他在方法类论文方面仅有的覆盖。对于理论论文，他指出"a common variation of the IMRAD structure separates the theory...into its own preceding section."

## 来源 B：Gastel & Day，《How to Write and Publish a Scientific Paper》（第 8 版，2016）

已读章节：Ch4（何为科学论文）、Ch7（标题）、Ch9（摘要）、Ch10（引言）、Ch11（方法与材料）、Ch12（结果）、Ch13（讨论）、Ch23（综述论文）、Ch35（学位论文）、Ch37（基金申请书）。

### B.1 何为科学论文（Ch. 4）

CBE/CSE 定义：一篇合格的原始科学出版物必须是**首次披露**，包含**充分信息**以供同行（1）评估观察结果，（2）重复实验，（3）评估智力过程。它必须"susceptible to sensory perception, essentially permanent, available to the scientific community without restriction, and available for regular screening by one or more of the major recognized secondary services." 会议报告、学位论文和政府文献通常不符合资格。"If a scientific paper meets all the other tests, it is not validly published if it is published in the wrong place."

IMRAD 逻辑：研究了什么问题？（引言）如何研究的？（方法）发现了什么？（结果）这些发现意味着什么？（讨论）

### B.2 标题（Ch. 7）

"A good title is the fewest possible words that adequately describe the contents of the paper." 标题是标签，不是句子。错误的句法（词序）是"the most common error in defective titles, and certainly the most damaging one in terms of comprehension." 标题将被数千人阅读；论文被少数人阅读。应避免的废词："Studies on," "Investigations on," "Observations on," 以及开头的冠词（A, An, The）。Rosner 的"assertive sentence title"（AST）——带动词的完整句子标题——通常应避免，因为它"boldly states a conclusion that is then stated more tentatively in the summary." 关于索引："An improperly titled paper may be virtually lost and never reach its intended audience."

### B.3 摘要（Ch. 9）

"An abstract should be viewed as a miniature version of the paper." 摘要应提供每个主要部分（引言、方法与材料、结果、讨论）的简要总结。四个组成部分：（1）陈述主要目的和范围，（2）描述所采用的方法，（3）总结结果，（4）陈述主要结论。大部分或全部应使用过去时。摘要绝不应给出任何论文中未陈述的信息或结论。不得引用文献（极少数例外）。通常不应引用表格和图表。

区分的两种类型：
- **信息型摘要（informative abstract）**：浓缩论文——陈述问题、方法、主要数据和结论。有时可替代阅读全文。用作研究论文的"heading"摘要。"However, before citing a paper, you should read it in its entirety because some abstracts — surely not yours, though! — do not convey an entirely accurate picture of the research."
- **指示型摘要（indicative/descriptive abstract）**：指示论文涉及的主题，类似目录。不应用作研究论文的 heading 摘要，但可用于综述论文、会议报告和政府报告。

McGirr (1973): "When writing the abstract, remember that it will be published by itself, and should be self-contained. That is, it should contain no bibliographic, figure, or table references." 先写论文，再写摘要。词语经济至关重要："E=mc²" 是有史以来最短的摘要。"If you cannot make a good impression in your abstract, your cause may be lost. Very often, the reviewer may be perilously close to a final judgment of your manuscript after reading the abstract alone." "Usually, a good abstract is followed by a good paper; a poor abstract is a harbinger of woes to come."

关于悬念："Reading a scientific article isn't the same as reading a detective story. We want to know from the start that the butler did it"（Ratnoff 1981）。

### B.4 引言（Ch. 10）

引言应提供足够的背景，使读者无需查阅先前出版物就能理解和评估结果。它应呈现问题及其解决方案。好的引言遵循**漏斗格式**：从一般到特殊。

Gastel & Day 提供的规则：（1）呈现问题的性质和范围，（2）综述相关文献，（3）陈述调查方法（及选择该方法的理由），（4）陈述主要结果，（5）陈述主要结论。"The introduction provides a road map from problem to solution. This map is so important that a bit of redundancy with the abstract is often desirable."

例外：某些学科的期刊偏好详尽的文献综述，几乎类似论文内嵌的综述文章。一些期刊将其作为引言之后的独立章节。学科惯例和目标期刊要求优先。定义专业术语和缩写。

### B.5 材料与方法（Ch. 11）

"Enough information must be given so that the experiments could be reproduced by a competent colleague." 一个好的检验：将完成的稿件副本交给一位同事，问他或她是否能理解该方法。

关键指导：
- 如果方法是新的（未发表），提供所有必要细节。
- 如果已在期刊上发表，给出文献引用。对于读者熟知的方法，仅需引用。对于读者可能不熟悉的方法，值得加上几句描述。
- "If several alternative methods are commonly employed, it is useful to identify your method briefly as well as to cite the reference."
- 统计分析："your paper should emphasize the data, not the statistics." 普通统计方法无需评论即可使用；高级或不寻常的方法可能需要文献引用。
- 为微生物菌株/突变体准备菌株表，为化合物准备性质表。
- 不要将结果包含在此部分中。"There is only one rule for a properly written materials and methods section: Enough information must be given so that the experiments could be reproduced by a competent colleague."
- 关于被动语态：在方法部分，被动语态通常可有效使用，因为"although what was done must be specified, who did it is often irrelevant."
- 最常见错误："to state the action without, when necessary, stating the agent of the action"——例如"To determine its respiratory quotient, the organism was..."（生物体无法做出这种决定）。

### B.6 结果（Ch. 12）

结果部分是论文最重要的部分——它构成所贡献的新知识。"The whole paper must stand or fall on the basis of the results. Thus, the results must be presented with crystal clarity." 引爱因斯坦："If you are out to describe the truth, leave elegance to the tailor."

关键指导：
- "The fool collects facts; the wise man selects them"（John Wesley Powell）。呈现代表性数据，而非全部数据。
- 避免冗余：最常见的错误是在文字中重复图表中已经显而易见的内容。更糟糕的是：在正文中呈现表格中的所有或许多数据。
- 引用图表时要简洁。不要说"It is clearly shown in Table 1 that nocillin inhibited the growth." 而说"Nocillin inhibited the growth (Table 1)."
- 定义负面方面："it is often good insurance to state what you did not find under the conditions of your experiments."
- 如果使用统计学，应当是有意义的统计学。
- 关于在线补充材料：越来越多的期刊在线上发布论文的补充材料。查阅期刊的投稿说明。

### B.7 讨论（Ch. 13）

"The discussion is harder to define than the other sections. Thus, it is usually the hardest section to write. And, whether you know it or not, many papers are rejected by journal editors because of a faulty discussion, even though the data of the paper might be both valid and interesting."

关键指导：
- 许多讨论部分过长且啰嗦。Doug Savile 的"squid technique"："the author is doubtful about his facts or his reasoning and retreats behind a protective cloud of ink."
- 避免更啰嗦的句式：说"I found that..."或"We conclude that..."而非"It was found in the present investigation that..."
- 陈述结果的含义。不要重述结果。讨论是解释；结果是呈现。
- "Try to present the principles, relationships, and generalizations shown by the Results. And bear in mind, in a good Discussion, you discuss — you do not recapitulate — the Results."
- 指出任何例外或缺乏相关性之处，并界定未解决的问题。展示你的结果和解释如何与先前发表的工作一致或对比。
- 讨论理论含义和可能的实际应用。尽可能清晰地陈述你的结论。总结每条结论的证据。

### B.8 综述论文（Ch. 23）

"A review paper is not an original publication in the usual sense, though it can be valuable scholarship." 通常 10–50 个出版页。"The really good review papers are much more than annotated bibliographies. They offer critical evaluation of the published literature and often provide important conclusions based on that literature."

组织方式不同于 IMRAD："Greatly expand the introduction; delete the materials and methods (unless original data are being presented or you will say how you identified and chose the literature to include); delete the results; and expand the discussion." 一条基本规则：准备提纲。"If your review is organized properly, the overall scope of the review will be well defined."

综述类型：
- **批判性评估综述**：老牌综述期刊"prefer, and some demand, authoritative and critical evaluations."
- **汇编/注释综述**："Annual Review of"系列——"designed to compile and to annotate but not necessarily to evaluate."
- **历史综述**：按时间顺序组织；"there is still a place for history."
- **现状综述**："provide a new understanding of a rapidly moving field."
- **系统综述**：针对"a clearly formulated question"，使用"systematic and explicit methods to identify, select, and critically appraise relevant research." 包含方法部分（搜索的数据库、搜索词、日期/语言、纳入/排除标准）。引用 PRISMA 声明。

受众：同行、相关领域人士和学生都会阅读。"Your style of writing should be much less technical than for a research paper. Jargon and specialized abbreviations must be eliminated or carefully explained."

### B.9 学位论文（Ch. 35）

目的："to prove that the candidate can do and communicate such research." 不同于科学论文，学位论文可以涉及多个主题。"The concept that a thesis must be a bulky 200-page tome is wrong, dead wrong. Many 200-page theses contain only 50 pages of good science."

欧洲 vs. 美国学位论文：欧洲学位论文"designed to show that the candidate has reached maturity and can both do science and write science. Such theses may be submitted after some years of work and a number of primary publications, with the thesis itself being a 'review paper' that brings it all together."

结构：若干章，有时基于 IMRAD，有时每章对应一项研究。常见组成部分：标题页、致谢、摘要、目录、图表列表、缩写列表、附录。"The introduction or a separate literature-review chapter generally should present a thorough review of previous work...it is often desirable to go back into the history of your subject."

时间安排："Allow at least 6 months." 在截止日期之前很久就开始写作。不是从开头写到结尾——尽早开始文献综述，在使用方法后尽快起草方法部分，引言通常最后起草。"A good thesis is likely to require multiple drafts."

学位论文本身不可发表："Extracting one or more publications from a thesis generally entails considerable trimming and condensation."

### B.10 基金申请书（Ch. 37）

"A good proposal generally includes background information relating to the proposed endeavor, a statement of goals, a research plan, a budget, and information about the qualifications of those who are to do the work." 如果篇幅较长：标题页、摘要、递交函、目录、预期影响描述、成果传播计划、设施信息、参考文献。

预申请书/意向书：可以节省工作并帮助塑造研究方向。需要"much of the same rigorous thinking"与完整申请书相同。常见部分：背景、目标、研究/项目计划、预算、简历。

关键指导：
- "Only if a proposal matches the priorities of a funding source is it likely to be funded." 咨询工作人员（项目官员）。查看成功申请书的范例。"In preparing grant proposals, as in other scientific writing, following good models saves time, avoids guesswork, and promotes success."
- 在截止日期前很久开始——对于长篇申请书至少提前 6 个月。
- "Read all instructions carefully, and follow them precisely." 不符合要求的申请书可能未经审查就被取消资格。
- "Match the technical level of the proposal to the background of the reviewers." 政府机构：较为专业。有外行董事会的私人资助来源：不超过科普文章的技术水平。
- 所有关键信息应出现在正文中；审稿人通常没有义务阅读附录。

## 来源 C：Whitesides，"Whitesides' Group: Writing a Paper"（*Advanced Materials*, 2004, 16, No. 15, August 4）

全文通过图像分析读取，共三页。

### C.1 何为科学论文？

"A paper is an organized description of hypotheses, data and conclusions, intended to instruct the reader. Papers are a central part of research. If your research does not generate papers, it might just as well not have been done. 'Interesting and unpublished' is equivalent to 'non-existent'."

"Realize that your objective in research is to formulate and test hypotheses, to draw conclusions from these tests, and to teach these conclusions to others. Your objective is not to 'collect data'."

论文也是"a structure for planning your research in progress. If you clearly understand the purpose and form of a paper, it can be immensely useful to you in organizing and conducting your research. A good outline for the paper is also a good plan for the research program."

### C.2 提纲方法

"I emphasize the central place of an outline in writing papers, preparing seminars, and planning research. I especially believe that for you, and for me, it is most efficient to write papers from outlines."

**定义**："a written plan of the organization of a paper, including the data on which it rests. You should, in fact, think of an outline as a carefully organized and presented set of data, with attendant objectives, hypotheses, and conclusions, rather than an outline of text."

**为何提纲有效**："An outline itself contains little text. If you and I can agree on the details of the outline (that is, on the data and organization), the supporting text can be assembled fairly easily. If we do not agree on the outline, any text is useless. Much of the time in writing a paper goes into the text; most of the thought goes into the organization of the data and into the analysis."

**迭代**："It can be relatively efficient in time to go through several (even many) cycles of an outline before beginning to write text; writing many versions of the full text of a paper is slow."

### C.3 如何构建提纲

从一张白纸开始。以任意顺序写下所有重要想法。问自己："Why did I do this work?"; "What does it mean?"; "What hypotheses did I mean to test?"; "What ones did I actually test?"; "What were the results?"; "What measurements did I make?"; "What compounds? How were they characterized?"; "Sketch possible equations, figures, and schemes."

然后将它们组织成三大堆：（1）引言，（2）结果与讨论，（3）结论。在每一部分内，"concentrate on organizing the data. Construct figures, tables, and schemes to present the data as clearly and compactly as possible. This process can be slow — I may sketch a figure five to ten times in different ways trying to decide how it is most clear."

**时机**："Do not, under any circumstances, wait until the collection of data is 'complete' before starting to write an outline. No project is ever complete."

**与导师的迭代**："It usually takes four to five iterations (often with additional experiments) to agree on an outline."

### C.4 提纲应包含什么

1. **标题**
2. **作者**
3. **摘要**——"Do not write an abstract. That can be done when the paper is complete."
4. **引言**——"The first paragraph or two should be written out completely. Pay particular attention to the opening sentence. Ideally, it should state concisely the objective of the work, and indicate why this objective is important." 要素：目的；理由；背景（谁做了什么）；读者指引（应关注什么）；总结。
5. **结果与讨论**——通常合并。按主题组织，配以粗体小标题。章节标题应"as specific and information-rich as possible," 例如"The Rate of Self-Exchange Decreases with the Polarity of the Solvent"而非"Measurement of Rates."
6. **结论**——"Summarize the conclusions as a list of short phrases or sentences. Do not repeat what is in the Results section. The Conclusions section should be just that, and not a summary. It should add a new, higher level of analysis, and should indicate explicitly the significance of the work."
7. **实验**——"Include, in the correct order to correspond to the order in the Results section, all of the paragraph subheadings."

### C.5 总结原则

- 尽早开始提纲。"Do not wait until the 'end'. The end may never come."
- "Organize the outline and the paper around easily assimilated data — tables, equations, figures, schemes — rather than around text."
- "Organize in order of importance, not in chronological order." 新手倾向于给出"a recitation of their experimental program, starting with their cherished initial failures and leading up to a climactic successful finale. This approach is completely wrong. Start with the most important results."
- "Shorter papers are easier to read than longer ones."

### C.6 风格要点

- 不要将名词用作形容词："ATP formation" → "formation of ATP"
- "This"后面必须跟名词："This reaction is fast"而非"This is a fast reaction"
- 实验结果统一使用过去时："Addition of water gave product"而非"gives"
- 尽可能使用主动语态："The solution turned red"而非"It was observed that the solution turned red"
- 完成所有比较："higher using bromine than chlorine"而非"higher using bromine"
- 双倍行距；冒号和句末句号后留两个空格；留宽裕页边距。

### C.7 覆盖范围
